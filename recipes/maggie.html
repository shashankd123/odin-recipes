Maggie

Maggie is easy to make recipy. It is cheap and tasty so most indian households uses it for quick snack

Ingredients

  • Maggie
  • Water
  • *Oil
  • *Vegitables
  • *Cheese
  • *Spices

Steps to make Maggie

  1. Add 1 glass of water
  2. Boil water
  3. Add the maggie brick
  4. Add maggie masala
  5. Boil for 5 minuts
